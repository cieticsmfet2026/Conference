ICSMFET2026

The International Conference on Sustainable Materials and Future Engineering Technologies provides a premier global forum for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policy-makers to exchange knowledge and insights on emerging innovations in sustainable materials and advanced engineering technologies. The conference aims to address pressing global challenges by promoting environmentally responsible, technologically advanced, and economically viable engineering solutions that support long-term sustainability goals.

The conference will showcase cutting-edge research and practical advancements across sustainable materials, smart and functional materials, nanomaterials, green and bio-based composites, and eco-efficient manufacturing processes. Emphasis is placed on the development and application of materials that enhance energy efficiency, durability, and environmental compatibility across diverse engineering domains, including civil, mechanical, electrical, and industrial systems.

Key thematic areas include biodegradable and recyclable materials, composite and hybrid material systems, materials for renewable energy generation, energy storage and conversion technologies, and materials designed for enhanced structural performance and resilience. In parallel, the conference highlights the growing integration of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data-driven approaches in materials design, performance prediction, and intelligent manufacturing, accelerating innovation in future engineering technologies.

A strong focus is dedicated to next-generation engineering practices, encompassing low-carbon technologies, circular economy principles, life-cycle assessment methodologies, sustainable infrastructure development, and resource-efficient system design. Technical sessions will explore resilient and adaptive engineering solutions aligned with climate resilience, regulatory frameworks, and global sustainable development agendas.

The conference also addresses the role of digital transformation in shaping future engineering technologies, covering topics such as digital twins, smart simulation environments, intelligent optimization tools, and cloud–edge-enabled engineering platforms. Through keynote lectures, technical sessions, panel discussions, and hands-on workshops, participants will gain insights into emerging tools, methodologies, and real-world applications transforming sustainable engineering practices

Industry participation, policy engagement, and funding perspectives form an integral component of the conference, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, technology transfer, start-up incubation, and commercialization of research outcomes. The conference actively promotes academic–industry partnerships and highlights successful case studies demonstrating impactful deployment of sustainable materials and engineering technologies.

By encouraging inclusive dialogue, interdisciplinary collaboration, and forward-looking innovation, the International Conference on Sustainable Materials and Future Engineering Technologies seeks to advance the frontiers of sustainable materials research and engineering practice. The conference serves as a catalyst for global knowledge exchange, uniting stakeholders to develop intelligent, resilient, and sustainable engineering technologies for the future.

The International Conference on Applied Materials and Future Sustainable Engineering Solutions (ICAMFSE-2026) invites original research articles, innovative case studies, and visionary proposals from researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policy-makers worldwide. The conference provides a multidisciplinary platform to present recent advances in applied materials, sustainable engineering practices, and emerging technologies that support global sustainability goals and industrial transformation.

ICAMFSE-2026 encourages high-quality contributions that bridge fundamental research and real-world engineering applications, fostering collaboration across academia, industry, and policy domains.

Paper Submission

The Organizing Committee of ICAMFSE-2026 invites researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and scientists from universities, R&D organizations, and industrial sectors to submit original and high-quality research papers presenting recent advances in applied materials, sustainable technologies, and engineering systems. All submissions must represent unpublished work, be original in content, and must not be under consideration for publication elsewhere.

General Submission Instructions

  • Manuscripts must be submitted to only one relevant session. Duplicate submissions across multiple tracks will be disqualified.
  • Only full-length research papers will be considered. Review articles, short communications, or previously published content are not permitted.
  • All authors are encouraged to adhere strictly to submission guidelines and deadlines to ensure a smooth review process.

Formatting and Structure

  • Manuscripts must comply with the official ICAMFSE-2026 paper template.
  • The maximum length of a submitted paper is 8 pages, inclusive of all tables, figures, and references.
  • Required sections: Title, Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Methodology, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, Acknowledgments, and References.

Contribution to Conference Theme

  • Submissions must clearly articulate how the research contributes to the themes of material innovation and sustainable engineering.
  • Emphasis will be placed on scientific rigor, novelty, and applicability to real-world engineering problems.

Originality and Plagiarism Policy

  • Every submission will be screened using plagiarism detection tools:
    • Similarity Index ≤ 15%: Eligible for review
    • Similarity Index 16–30%: Sent back for revision
    • Similarity Index > 30%: Rejected outright
  • All citations and references must be properly included to uphold academic integrity.

Review Process

  • Each manuscript will undergo a double-blind peer review by at least two expert reviewers.
  • Evaluation criteria include:
    • ✔ Originality & Technical Depth
    • ✔ Relevance to ICAMFSE Themes
    • ✔ Clarity of Presentation
    • ✔ Research Impact and Innovation

Revisions and Final Submission

  • Papers that receive conditional acceptance must be revised in line with reviewer comments and resubmitted within the given timeframe.
  • Authors must submit the final version along with copyright and registration formalities.

Plagiarism Policy

  • Plagiarism Check: All submitted papers will be checked for plagiarism using standard tools. References and author details will be excluded from the check.
  • Acceptable Limit: Papers with less than 15% similarity will be accepted for review.
  • Needs Revision: Papers with 16% to 20% similarity will be returned to the authors for correction and resubmission.
  • Automatic Rejection: Papers with more than 20% similarity will be rejected without review.

Review Policy

  • Double-Blind Review: Papers will undergo a double-blind peer review, where reviewers do not know the identity of authors and authors do not know the identity of reviewers.
  • Review Process: Each paper will be reviewed by at least two independent reviewers. The review process will evaluate originality, technical quality, relevance to the conference theme, and clarity of presentation.
  • Reviewer Criteria: Reviewers must be PhD holders affiliated with reputable institutions. Those interested in becoming reviewers can send a request to the conference chair.
  • Certificate for Reviewers: All reviewers will receive an honorary certificate for their contribution, regardless of the final decision on the papers.

Acceptance Policy

  • Acceptance Notification: Authors of accepted papers will be notified through the paper submission portal. Papers will be accepted based on originality, research quality, clarity, and relevance to the conference themes.
  • Revisions: Accepted papers may be requested to undergo minor revisions based on reviewer comments. Authors are required to submit revised papers within the specified timeline to complete the acceptance process.

Registration Policy

  • Registration Requirement: At least one author of each accepted paper must register for ICAMFSE 2026 to have their paper included in the conference proceedings and presentation schedule.
  • Registration Fee: The registration fee covers the conference kit, presentation slot, participation certificate, and access to conference proceedings.

Withdrawal Policy

  • During Review Process: Authors may withdraw their paper at any point before a decision has been made on the review.
  • After Acceptance: For accepted papers, withdrawal is possible with the following refund policy

Presentation Policy

  • Presentation: All authors of accepted papers are required to present their paper at the conference. The standard format is a 15-minute PowerPoint presentation with 12 minutes for presenting and 3 minutes for questions and answers.
  • Offline Presentations: ICAMFSE 2026 will be conducted primarily in offline mode. However, under special circumstances, authors may request permission to present online, but this is subject to approval by the conference authorities.
  • Online Presentations: Requests for online presentations must be accompanied by a valid reason and approval is not guaranteed for all cases.

Certificate Policy

  • Participation Certificates:All participants in ICAMFSE 2026 will receive a printed certificate of participation.
  • Presentation Certificates: Authors who present their papers will receive a certificate of presentation.
  • Reviewer Certificates: Reviewers will be awarded an honorary certificate (soft copy) for their contribution to the review process, regardless of the outcome of the papers they reviewed.

Camera Ready Submission Policy

  • Camera Ready Submission:Authors of all presented papers must submit the camera ready version of their work within the prescribed time.
  • Publication Format: Papers must follow the specific guidelines and format required by the respective publications.

Publication Policy

  • Proceedings Publication:High-quality papers presented at ICAMFSE 2026 will be submitted to the respective publication partners for potential publication.
  • Publication Guidelines: Papers must meet the requirements set by the proceedings to be eligible for publication. The final decision on publication will be made by the respective editors or authorized representatives of the publication.
  • Publication Timeline:The timeline for publication will depend on the processes followed by each publication partner.

All high quality accepted and presented papers will be submitted to Scopus indexed Proceedings for publication.
